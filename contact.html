CEO’s Message

Saansud’s name has stood out despite a sluggish market. Our residential success is ongoing, but our commercial success is just beginning.

Customer confidence is key to our continuous growth. I am convinced that our integrity and honesty are the only things that can make us a winner. As a group, we have always lived by the dictate to promise less and deliver more. Our business is based on certain core values, which we reinforce to face tough challenges.

As we see trends today, affordable housing is the need of the hour and you can see many developers who were in the luxury or high‑end segment making their foray into affordable homes. We are happy to say that we have made our foray with Laxmi Narayan Vihar, our flagship affordable homes in a futuristic location, Paradeep. I truly believe that the challenge we face is to execute the projects as early as possible, to gain profits and the availability of reasonably priced land in urban areas.

We bring strong knowledge and research capability, focus on goal‑orientedness, and carry good team spirit. We rope in opportunities at the right time, have a strong customer focus and continuous efforts to deliver the best.

Sitanshu Mohapatra CEO — Saansud Group
