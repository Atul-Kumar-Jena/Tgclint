Who we are

A studio built

We started with a simple conviction: that glass deserves the same rigour as steel and the same care as fine joinery. Two decades on, that conviction shapes everything — from the way we draw a junction to the way we protect a site.

Our workshop in Shoreditch is where ideas are tested at full scale before they ever reach a build. It is also where we welcome architects to see, touch and understand the systems we make.
