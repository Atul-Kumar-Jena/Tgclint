01

Building construction

From the first 2D floor plan to the day you get the keys, one accountable team carries the build — structural drawings, working drawings, site execution and every approval in between.

  • 2D floor plans & 3D elevations
  • Structural & working drawings for execution
  • Plumbing, electrical & furniture layouts
  • Fe500/550 steel · RMC M20/M25 concrete
  • Soil testing before design begins
  • 10-year structural warranty

How it runs

  1. Design & budgeting — plans, materials and costing fixed before ground is broken
  2. Execution — milestone-based construction with our own supervisors on site
  3. Finishing — full quality review and snag resolution before handover
  4. Handover — documented warranty and post-handover support

Interior designing

Kitchens, wardrobes, lighting and finishes — designed and delivered with the same transparency as our construction, at a flat design fee regardless of budget or scale.

  • Space planning and 3D visualisation
  • Modular kitchen and wardrobe design
  • Lighting design and electrical co-ordination
  • Flat design fee — no percentage mark-ups

How it runs

  1. Consultation — lifestyle, budget and style preferences mapped out
  2. Design — full 3D layout and material palette presented for approval
  3. Execution — modular fit-out by our own execution team
  4. Handover — final walkthrough and finishing touches

03

Renovation & remodel

Reflooring, painting, kitchen and bathroom upgrades and full remodelling — planned around your life, with minimal disruption and a clear material plan from day one.

  • Structural assessment before any work begins
  • Kitchen and bathroom upgrades
  • Reflooring and wall treatments
  • Phased work plans to limit household disruption

How it runs

  1. Assessment — site survey and scope agreed in writing
  2. Planning — material selection and a phased schedule
  3. Execution — work carried out in agreed phases
  4. Sign-off — final inspection and clean handover

04

Architecture & approvals

Site-specific design paired with full government liaison — sanctioned plans, electricity, water and sewage connections handled so you never have to navigate any of it yourself.

  • Site-specific architectural design
  • Construction plan sanction (BDA/CDA/Panchayat/Tahasil)
  • Temporary & permanent electricity connection
  • Water and sewage connection assistance

How it runs

  1. Site study — orientation, soil and local regulation review
  2. Design — layout drawn for the site, not a generic template
  3. Sanction — drawings filed and approved with local authorities
  4. Connections — utility connections arranged ahead of handover
