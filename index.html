Building construction

Bare plot to handover keys — one accountable team, one fixed price, a 10-year warranty in writing. No subcontractor blame-games. No budget surprises.

  • 2D floor plans & 3D elevations
  • Structural & working drawings for execution
  • Plumbing, electrical & furniture layouts
  • Fe500/550 steel · RMC M20/M25 concrete
  • Soil testing before design begins
  • 10-year structural warranty

How it runs

  1. Design & budgeting — plans, materials and costing fixed before ground is broken
  2. Execution — milestone-based construction with our own supervisors on site
  3. Finishing — full quality review and snag resolution before handover
  4. Handover — documented warranty and post-handover support

04

Architecture & approvals

Designed for your site, sanctioned by us. You sign once — we handle the drawings, the approvals and every utility connection.

  • Site-specific architectural design
  • Construction plan sanction (BDA/CDA/Panchayat/Tahasil)
  • Temporary & permanent electricity connection
  • Water and sewage connection assistance

How it runs

  1. Site study — orientation, soil and local regulation review
  2. Design — layout drawn for the site, not a generic template
  3. Sanction — drawings filed and approved with local authorities
  4. Connections — utility connections arranged ahead of handover
